A warm welcome at our new home

Members of every age and ability gather every week at the Kidderminster Harriers Social Club. Whether you are returning to the game after years away or sitting down at a board for the very first time, you will find a friendly face and a fair match.

When

Every Monday

7:00 PM. We play chess all year round. The league season runs from September to May.

Where

Kidderminster Harriers Social Club

Stadium Close, Hoo Road, Kidderminster · DY10 1NB · Free parking.

Cost

Free to play

No chess club fee. Venue membership is £6 for six months, plus a £1 application fee. ECF membership for rated league play.

Your move.

Whether it is your first game or your thousandth, there is a seat at the board for you.
